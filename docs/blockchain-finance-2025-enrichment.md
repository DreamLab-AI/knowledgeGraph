### Regional CBDC Progress

**1. Europe (22 countries)**
- **Leaders**: Sweden, France, Germany

- **Focus**: Privacy-preserving design, offline functionality

**2. Asia-Pacific (19 countries)**
- **Leaders**: China, India, Singapore, Japan
- **China e-CNY**: 7 trillion yuan ($986 billion) transaction volume across 17 provinces
- **Use Cases**: Education, healthcare, tourism (nearly 4x growth from 2023)

**3. Africa (19 countries)**
- **Focus**: Financial inclusion, cross-border remittances
- **Adoption**: Mobile-first CBDC designs for unbanked populations

**4. Caribbean (12 nations)**
- **Leaders**: Bahamas (Sand Dollar), Jamaica (JAM-DEX)
- **Status**: Fully operational retail CBDCs
- **Emphasis**: Financial inclusion, reduced cash dependency

**5. United States**

- **Rationale**: Privacy concerns, banking sector protection
- **Unique**: Only major economy to halt CBDC development

### CBDC Regulatory Frameworks 2025

- **48 Countries**: Aligned with FATF AML/CFT standards

- **72% of Central Banks**: Collaborating with private fintech firms

**Key Regulatory Challenges:**
1. **Privacy vs. Surveillance**: Balancing transparency with user privacy
